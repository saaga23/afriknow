# AI-Annotation Ethics Statement

## Disclosure

All annotations (predictions and confidence estimates) in this study were generated by seven large language models via API. No human annotators were involved in the labeling process.

## Implications

We acknowledge that AI-generated confidence estimates reflect model-internal representations rather than human metacognition. The term "confidence" in this paper refers to **verbalized confidence estimation (VCE)** produced by LLMs, not to human introspective confidence reports.

## Limitations

- AI confidence calibration may not generalize to human calibration patterns.
- The models used are predominantly trained on English-language and Western-centric data, which may affect their confidence estimation on non-Western content.
- No human validation arm was conducted due to the fully automated, no-budget annotation protocol.

## Compliance

This study does not involve human subjects research and therefore does not require IRB review. All data is sourced from publicly available benchmarks (Global-MMLU).
